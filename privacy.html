SlotBot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SlotBot ("the Bot") collects, stores, and uses information necessary to provide its features and services on Discord.

Information We Collect

SlotBot may store the following information:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Virtual currency balances
  • Game statistics and progression data
  • Bot configuration settings
  • Command usage required for bot functionality

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

  • Provide SlotBot's economy and game systems
  • Store user balances and progression
  • Track game results and statistics
  • Save server-specific settings and preferences
  • Prevent abuse, cheating, or exploitation
  • Maintain and improve bot functionality

Data Sharing

SlotBot does not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed when required by applicable law or legal process.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the Bot and its features. Data may be deleted at any time at the discretion of the Bot owner.

User Requests

Users may request the deletion of their stored data by contacting the Bot owner through Discord. Reasonable efforts will be made to fulfill valid requests.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information; however, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

SlotBot is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements. The Bot does not knowingly collect personal information beyond Discord identifiers required for operation.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Bot owner through Discord.
